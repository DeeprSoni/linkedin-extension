================================================================================

â€¢ Use bullet points (very scannable)
â€¢ Lead with most impactful changes
â€¢ Use action words: "Fixed", "Added", "Improved"
â€¢ Be specific but concise
â€¢ Group similar changes
â€¢ Include version number
â€¢ Add contact email if space allows

DON'T:
â€¢ Write paragraphs (too dense)
â€¢ Use technical jargon
â€¢ List every tiny change
â€¢ Be vague ("various improvements")
â€¢ Exceed 500 characters
â€¢ Use emojis excessively

â€¢ Absolute max: 500 characters
â€¢ Count includes spaces and punctuation
