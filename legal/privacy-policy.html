Privacy Policy for LinkedIn Connection Scanner

Last Updated: [Date]

Overview

LinkedIn Connection Scanner is a Chrome extension that helps you scan and organize your 2nd degree LinkedIn connections for outreach and relationship management. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Key Point: All your data is stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or have access to your data.

Data We Collect

The extension collects and stores the following information locally on your device only:

1. LinkedIn Profile Information

  • Name
  • Headline
  • Current company
  • Location
  • Number of mutual connections
  • Profile URL
  • Scan timestamp

2. User-Generated Content

  • Status labels you assign to prospects (e.g., "new", "contacted", "replied")
  • Notes you write about prospects
  • Tags you create and assign
  • Message templates you create
  • Message campaign data

3. Usage Statistics

  • Total number of profiles scanned
  • Last scan date
  • Count of new prospects found

4. Extension Settings

  • Priority scoring preferences
  • Auto-click preferences for message buttons

How We Use Your Data

All data is used exclusively for:

  1. Display and Organization: Showing you scanned LinkedIn profiles in an organized format
  2. Search and Filter: Enabling you to search, sort, and filter your prospect list
  3. Export: Allowing you to export your data to CSV format
  4. Message Tracking: Tracking outreach campaigns and messaging activity

Where Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's local storage API.

  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • No data is accessible to the extension developers
  • Data remains on your device unless you explicitly export it

Data Retention

Data is retained locally on your device indefinitely until you:

  • Delete individual prospects through the extension interface
  • Clear all data using the extension's "Clear All" function
  • Uninstall the extension (which automatically removes all local data)

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

Access

You can view all stored data through the extension interface at any time.

Export

Use the "Export to CSV" function to download your data in a portable format.

Delete

  • Delete individual prospects by clicking the delete button in the extension
  • Clear all data at once using the "Clear All" function in settings
  • Uninstall the extension to remove all stored data

Third-Party Services

This extension interacts with LinkedIn.com:

  • The extension reads publicly visible information from LinkedIn pages you visit
  • It does NOT access LinkedIn's API
  • It does NOT log into LinkedIn on your behalf
  • It does NOT capture your LinkedIn credentials
  • It does NOT send automated messages or perform actions without your explicit input
